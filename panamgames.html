Feelings 💔

NOOOOOOOOOO

I think that athletes should speak out about issues that are important to them. Politics affects many aspects of sports, such as decisions over how anti-cheating rules are enforced, potential boycotts of games, who is allowed to play, and how athletes are treated. These specific issues directly affect athletes and their performance, so they should be speaking out. Furthermore, athletes are doing more than competing in games; they are public figures who represent a country. If a country is doing something harmful, not speaking out against it can be interpreted as a tacit endorsement of what is going on. Speaking out has real potential to make a difference and bring awareness to an important issue, even if some people may disagree.
